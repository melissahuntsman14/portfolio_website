About me

Hi, I am Melissa.

I am currently studying design at Massey University with a major in visual communcication design. I love to be creative when I get the chance, my favourite things to create and design are posters. I also love taking photos and videos in my spare time.

Summer is my favourite time of the year as that's when I usually am able to pull out my camera and go pro in order to get some footage. I love spending time with my friends, going on adventures and seeing my family. I prefer to be outdoors exploring new places or searching for views but I also like to be at home watching a good tv series with a bowl of ice cream.

At home I have three crazy brothers, a dog called Charlie and my Mum and Dad we love to spend time together watching the rugby, going out kayaking, going on bush walks, eating and playing board games. Sometimes I get my home mixed up with the zoo as it can get wild at times, but that's all part of the fun.

I want to travel the world and it has been a dream of mine since I was young. My dream destination would have to be Bora Bora or Hawaii or anywhere tropical! Another dream destination would have to be Europe, I think I would visit everywhere possible.
